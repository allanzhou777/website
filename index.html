Research

These are big boy projects, the longest of which took over a year. Only made possible through really insightful and wonderful teachers/mentors (and StackOverflow).

  • [Adversarial attacks] : In this 12+ month project, my mentor and I worked to build a robust adversarial ResNet18 transfer model in order to classify medical images, namely COVID-19 radiography, breast ultrasounds, and brain tumors. You can read the blog post (linked) to learn more about our methods and decisions.
  • [Hip Hop throughout the 2010s] : A data science research project that taught me web scraping, how to use the Spotify API, YouTube analytics, basic data analysis, and how to present visualizations through matplotlib, and other softwares. This project culminated in a ~5000 word research paper (linked) and a 20 minute presentation.
  • [Cyclic Base Orderings] : A summer research project my me and two other friends focused on cyclic base orderings, a specific type of spanning tree, and some of their properties.
  • [Archimedean solids] : A self inquiry project for Advanced Calculus III, in which I found the surface area of select Archimedean solids, such as the small rhombicuboctahedron or icosidodecahedron, using my generalized approach.
